  • Full name: "Ene-ken-ti-Oba"
  • Name Origin : a bird from Onitsha, Anambra state, Nigeria
  • Game Designer: Zuby Onwuta
  • Name Provided by : Zome Onabamiro

Kenti an IOS single player game, designed and developed in under 3 days,
is a winner of the Apple WorldWide Developer Conference
2015 Minority Scholarship.
The Objective of the game is to free "Kenti", a bird trapped in a
cage so she can join her friends. The game is played in two modes
A)Hands on with double finger tap
B)Hands free with an EEG reader such as Neurosky Mobile headset.
The game is part of a larger project aimed at using games to
conduct a research on the "Sustained use of EEG in daily life"
Work is in progress to develop an app for this research using IOS
